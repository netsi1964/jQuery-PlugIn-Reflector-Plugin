jquery.plugInReflector.js

Version 0.01

This is a jQuery plug-in which can do some reflection on jQuerified elements. This can be very usefull when you try to explore possibilities of say image galleries.
plugInReflector will show you in a structured way the settings of such galleries. I will keep developing this plug-in.
Download it from jQuery-PlugIn-Reflector-Plugin.
